GOLA KABAB RECIPE

Ingredients

  • 1 pound beef or mutton mince
  • 1 medium onion,finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger paste
  • 1 tablespoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro (optional)
  • 2-3 green chilies, chopped (optional)

Instructions:

1. In a large bowl, mix together the mince, onion, garlic, ginger paste, coriander powder, cumin powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, salt, and lemon juice.

2. Mix well until everything is fully incorporated.

3. Cover the mixture and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.

4. When ready to cook, shape the mixture into small cylindrical shapes (golay).

5. Heat oil in a pan and shallow-fry the golay until they're golden brown and cooked through.

6. Serve hot with naan, rice, or as a snack with your favorite chutney!

Tips:

Make sure to mix the ingredients well and don't overmix the mince.
You can adjust the amount of chili powder to suit your desired level of spiciness.
Traditionally, Gola Kababs are cooked on skewers over a grill or in a tandoor. If you have access to one, feel free to try that method!
